This is a very interesting game that lets you use your wits to find out if a number is either divisible by 3 or by 5.

If a number is divisible by 3, "Ping" will be displayed, if it is divisible by 5,"Pong" will be displayed. If its divisible by both,"Ping-Pong"will be displayed, else, the number you input will be displayed to signify that its neither divisible by 3 nor 5

Enjoy the Game even as you play it!!

LETS PLAY NOW!!!

Rules:

Ping-Pong will count your number from 1 with the following exceptions

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become "Ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 become "Pong"
  3. Numbers divisible by both 3 and 5 become "PingPong"
